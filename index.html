The Work

We help organizations find their node — and align everything around it.

Since 2018, Learn Ubique has partnered with nonprofits, foundations, schools, government agencies, and mission-driven organizations across the country. We meet every client where they are — and build the systems, language, and confidence to get them where they want to go.

About

Jenay
Sermon,
PhD

Founder and principal of Learn Ubique LLC, Jenay Sermon is a researcher, evaluator, and organizational transformation consultant with over eight years of practice at the intersection of data, equity, and community impact.

Her work is grounded in the belief that every organization has a core node of purpose — and that when programs, data, and story are truly aligned to that node, impact becomes undeniable and fundable.

She brings a rare combination of doctoral rigor, cultural responsiveness, and deep community roots to every engagement.
